About

I’m a psycholinguist researching how L1 and L2 language readers comprehend and predict language in real time. I combine behavioral experiments (e.g., eye-tracking, self-paced reading, acceptability judgments) with computational models (e.g., probabilistic / surprisal-based predictors through large language models) to test psycholinguistic hypotheses about human language processing.
